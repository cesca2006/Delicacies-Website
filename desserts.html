No bake mini cheesecakes

    Ingredients

  • Crushed graham crackers
  • Butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Salt
  • Heavy cream
  • Cream cheese
  • Powdered sugar
  • Vanilla extract

    Instructions

  1. Mix together your graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, sugar, and salt.
  2. Press roughly 2 tablespoons crumbs into each paper liner to form a bottom crust.
  3. Make whipped cream by beating your heavy cream on high speed until it thickens and stiff peaks form.
    Transfer your whipped cream to another bowl and keep cold in the fridge.
  4. Beat together the cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla in the same mixing bowl until smooth and creamy.
  5. Fold in your whipped cream until no white streaks remain.
  6. Fill mini graham cracker crusts with cheesecake filling.
  7. Add toppings to your heart's desire!

Mini Raspberry Almond Tarts

    Ingredients

  • 1 Cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 Cup almond flour
  • 1/3 Cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 Tsp salt
  • 1/2 Cup butter, cubbed
  • 1/2 Tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/4 Tsp almond extract
  • 1/2 Cup raspberry jam
  • Desired fruits for toppings

    Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees and prepare a mini muffin pan,
    by lining the wells with mini paper muffin liners.
  2. In a food processor combine flour, almond flour, sugar,
    baking powder, salt, butter, vanilla extract, and almond extract.
    Pulse until the dough comes together, and then pour it out onto a
    clean surface and knead together until smooth.
  3. Use a level small spring-loaded cookie scoop to form tight dough balls,
    rolling them in the palms of your hands.
    Place the dough balls in the muffin liners.
  4. Use the blunt end of a wooden spoon to indent the top of each dough ball
    about 1/4 inch to make room for the raspberry jam.
    Spoon the raspberry preserves into a plastic zip top bag,
    then squeeze the air out and seal.
    Cut a tiny bit of one of the bottom corners off and
    pipe the jam into the indent in the top of the dough balls.
    The jam will sink slightly when baking,
    so overfill the indented space just a bit.
  5. Bake the tarts in a 350 degree oven for 13-15 minutes.
    Allow them to cool for about 20 minutes in the pan,
    or they will be too crumbly to remove easily.
  6. Use your fruits for toppings and make them pretty.
