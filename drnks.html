Strawberry Dirty Sprite

    Ingredients

  • 8 oz sprite/lemon-lime soda
  • 1 oz strawberry syrup
  • 1 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 drop vanilla extract

    Instructions

  1. Chill your glass. Fill a tall glass with ice.
    If you’ve got a few minutes, chill it in the freezer first.
    It makes every sip colder, longer.
  2. Mix the strawberry magic.
    In a shaker or mason jar, combine strawberry syrup, lime juice, and vanilla.
    Shake hard for 10 seconds. It’s joy in motion.
  3. Add your bubbles.
    Pour the fruity mix into your ice-filled glass.
    Gently top with Sprite. Stir softly so you keep the fizz.
  4. Add fresh strawberries, lime wedge, maybe a straw. Then watch it shine.

Electric Flamingo Drink

    Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup pink grapefruit juice
  • 1/2 cup orange juice
  • 1/4 cup pineapple juice
  • 1 tbsp grenadine syrup
  • 1/4 cup sparkling water
  • Ice
  • Pink sugar (for rim)
  • Orange wheel (for garnish)

    Instructions

  1. Moisten the rim of your tall glass with grapefruit juice.
    Dip it into pink sugar or citrus zest to
    create a colorful rim that adds flair.
  2. In a cocktail shaker or mixing glass:
    Combine ½ cup pink grapefruit juice, ½ cup orange juice,
    ¼ cup pineapple juice, and 1 tbsp grenadine.
    Shake gently until mixed well.
  3. Fill your prepared glass with ice.
    Pour the juice mixture over the ice,
    then top it off with ¼ cup sparkling water or club soda.
    Enjoy the fizz!
