Appetizers

Crab Cakes

    Ingredients

  • 2 Large eggs
  • 6 mayonnaise
  • 2 tbsp minced green onions
  • 8 tbsp lemon juice
  • 2 tsp dried tarragon
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 1 pound crabmeat
  • 1 cup crushes salted crackers
  • 2 tbsp butter

    Instructions

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Whisk egg, mayonnaise, green onions, lemon juice, tarragon,
    and pepper flakes together in a medium bowl.
  3. Gently stir in crabmeat, being careful not to break up meat.
  4. Gradually mix in cracker crumbs,
    adding until desired consistency is achieved.
  5. Form crab mixture into patties.
    Heat butter in a skillet over medium heat.
  6. Cook patties in the skillet until golden brown, 5 to 6 minutes on each side.
  7. Serve and enjoy!

Bruschetta

    Ingredients

  • 6 Tomatoes 11/2 lbs, diced
  • 1/3 cup basil leaves, chopped
  • 5 garlic cloves, divided
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 2 tbsp virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1 baguette
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/3 cup shredded parmesan cheese

    Instructions

  1. Core and dice tomatoes (or use a food chopper).
    Drain any excess juice and transfer tomatoes to a medium bowl.
    Chop Basil Stack basil leaves and roll them into a tube.
  2. Using a sharp knife, thinly slice the basil into ribbons
    and transfer to the bowl with tomatoes.
  3. mince 5 garlic cloves.
    Mix 1 tsp of minced garlic into 3 Tbsp olive oil and set aside.
  4. Add remaining minced garlic to the mixing bowl with tomatoes.
    Season tomatoes with 2 Tbsp olive oil, 1 Tbsp balsamic,
    1/2 tsp salt and 1/4 tsp black pepper.
    Stir gently to combine and set aside to marinate for 30 minutes.
  5. Preheat oven to 400˚F with a rack in the center of the oven.
    Place toasts on a parchment-lined baking sheet.
    Cut toasts into 1/2” thick slices, slicing diagonally.
  6. Arrange toasts on the prepared baking sheet,
    and brush the garlic infused olive oil on both sides of toasts.
  7. Sprinkle tops with parmesan cheese and bake at 400˚F for 5 minutes
    then broil on high heat for 2 minutes or until the edges are golden brown.
